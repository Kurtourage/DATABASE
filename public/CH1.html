CHAPTER 1

At the grand party in the Whitewood residence, where everyone wore a fancy masks and beautiful clothes, something terrible happened. Suddenly, screams filled the air as Lady Evelyn Whitewood cried out in fear and sadness. Everyone was startled and worried. Their masked faces showing how surprised and scared they were.

People started talking, wondering if it was an accident or if something bad had been done on purpose. Everyone hurried to the garden, and saw a sad scene, Mr. Marcus Whitewood and the head butler, Mr. Edmund, were lying on the ground, not moving at all. It made the happy party turn into a depressing place filled with tears and quite sadness.
